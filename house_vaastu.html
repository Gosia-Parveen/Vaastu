HOUSE VAASTU

House Vaastu plays a vital role in shaping the energy and well-being of a home. Rooted in ancient Indian traditions, Vaastu Shastra emphasizes the balance between the five elements—earth, water, fire, air, and space—and their influence on human life. In a Vaastu-compliant house, the entrance is placed in the most auspicious direction, usually east or north, to welcome prosperity and positive energy. Rooms are arranged thoughtfully: the kitchen in the southeast (fire element), the master bedroom in the southwest (stability), and the prayer or meditation room in the northeast (spiritual growth). Even the positioning of furniture, water sources, and windows is considered to ensure harmony and health. By following these principles, House Vaastu not only enhances peace, relationships, and financial stability but also connects modern homes with timeless cosmic energy, making the living space a true sanctuary.
